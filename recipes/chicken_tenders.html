Chicken Tenders

Description

Juicy chicken tenders marinated in a season buttermilk mixture and then deep-fried to perfection.

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 2 lbs. chicken tenderloins
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika

Breading

  • 1.5 cup all purpose flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper(optional)
  • 1 tsp ground plack pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 3 tbsp buttermilk

Cooking

  • Enough vegetable oil to fill your deep fryer

Steps

Marinade

  1. Combine buttermilk and seasonings (I usually do so inside the measuring cup for the buttermilk
  2. Add chicken tenders to a gallon size sealable bag
  3. Pour the buttermilk mixture in and seal the bag (leave as little air inside as possible)
  4. Massage the bag until the chicken is evenly coated
  5. Store in the fridge for at least 4 hours, although, marinading for at least 24 hours is preferable

Breading

Make the breading

  1. In a large bowl, combine all dry ingredients
  2. Whisk together until well blended
  3. Add buttermilk and stir with a fork until mixture is evenly clumpy

Bread the chicken

  1. Remove a tender from the bag and place it in the breading bowl
  2. Cover the tender completely with the breading
  3. Place aside for frying (I generally use a plate to stack them on)

Cooking

  1. Line a backing sheet with a few layers of paper towels and set near the deep fryer
  2. Pour oil in your deep fryer until it reaches at least the min line
  3. Heat the oil to 350Â° F
  4. Place an amount of tenders in the fry basket, try to avoid placing so many that they touch
  5. Fry until the tenders are floating and have a nice golden color
  6. Remove tenders from the basket and place on the lined baking sheet
  7. Continue frying in batches until you cook all the tenders
