Stuffed Shells

Description

Jumbo stuffed shells filled with cheese and topped with tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of jumbo pasta shells (12 oz)
  • 1 conainer of ricotta cheese (32 oz)
  • 1 lb. shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 8 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 jar pasta sauce (28 oz)
  • 8 oz sliced fresh mushrooms

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Brin a large pot of salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook al dente.
  3. In a larg bowl, mix ricotta cheese, half of the mozzarella, half of the parmesan, eggs, parsley, salt, and pepper until combined.
  4. Combine pasta saush and mushrooms in a medium bowl. Add the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Stir until combined.
  5. Stuff shells with ricotta mixture and place in a 9x13 baking dish. Pour sauce mixture over the shells.
  6. Bake in the oven until the edges are bubbly.
  7. Serve and enjoy.
