Grilled Cheese

Description

The classic grilled sandwich. Melted cheese between two grilled pieces of bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread per sandwich.
  • Mayonaise
  • 2 slices of cheese per sandwich.

Steps

  1. Spread mayonaise on one side of each slice of bread. Be sure to cover the bread fully in an even coat of mayonaise.
  2. Heat a frying pan to medium/medium-low depending on your range.
  3. Place one slice of bread mayonaise side down.
  4. Add your cheese slices to the bread slice that is in the pan.
  5. Top the sandwich with the other slice of bread, mayonaise side facing up.
  6. Cook the bottom until it is golden brown. Flip, then cook the bottom until golden brown.
  7. Serve and enjoy.
